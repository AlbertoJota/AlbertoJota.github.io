Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a breed of dog originating in Britain , was developed for hunting wildfowl.

  • Scientific Name: Canis familiares
  • Average Length: 51-61 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Habitat: Domestic

The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl such as ducks and upland game birds during hunting and shooting parties.
